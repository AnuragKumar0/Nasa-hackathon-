Explore Exo Planets

Exoplanet websites provide comprehensive platforms for exploring planets outside our solar system, offering a wealth of scientific data, visualizations, and interactive tools. These sites introduce exoplanets and explain detection methods like the transit and radial velocity methods, making the science accessible to both researchers and enthusiasts. They feature extensive databases with details on thousands of confirmed exoplanets, including their names, host stars, orbital characteristics, and potential for habitability. Many websites also offer interactive visualization tools that allow users to explore these distant worlds, enhancing the learning experience with dynamic, engaging content.
